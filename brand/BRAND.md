# Vela — Brand Guide

**Name:** Vela — the southern constellation "the sails." Chosen for the reel's "map of cinema / constellation of taste" idea, a real word (not an AI portmanteau), short/brandable, with a quiet Barcelona resonance (vela = sail/candle).

**Tagline:** "your personal map of cinema"

## Color (single accent + dark only)
| Role | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Accent (Vela Gold) | `#F2C14E` | CTAs, links, active nav, the constellation mark, highlights |
| Accent text-on-gold | `#3A2D00` | text placed on a gold fill |
| Background | `#0B0A12` | page background (deep near-black indigo) |
| Surface / card | `#16131F` | cards, panels, modals |
| Border | `#2A2540` | hairlines, dividers |
| Text primary | `#F6F4FF` | headings, body |
| Text muted | `#9A93B2` | secondary text, captions |

No light theme, no theme toggle. Everything is dark.

## Logo
- `logo-icon.svg` — square app icon / favicon (gold constellation "V" with a guiding-star glint on dark rounded square).
- `logo-lockup.svg` — horizontal mark + "vela" wordmark (transparent, for the navbar on dark).
- The mark is a 5-star constellation forming a "V" (Vela) with a 4-point glint on the top-left guiding star.

## Typography
- Wordmark: geometric sans, weight 600, slight letter-spacing (system stack `Helvetica Neue, Arial` in the asset; can upgrade to a Google font like Sora/Poppins in-app).
- UI: keep the template's clean sans; two weights (400/600).

## Voice
Cinephile but friendly. Lowercase wordmark "vela". Sentence case in UI.
